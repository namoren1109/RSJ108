How to Make a Successful Brand Logo

Assembly time: 30-60 minutes
Awesome rating: 550% awesome

Have you been finding yourself struggling with your brand logo? Want to make sure it will work for any format? Print or Web? Consistent on all your platforms? Easily recognizable? Follow the steps below and find out how to make your brand logo a success!

  • Things You'll Need

  • Laptop or Desktop
  • Adoble Illustrator
  • Time
  • Sketch paper
  • Pen
  • Water (NOT near your computer!)

  1. Start by sketching out different logo ideas on sketch paper.

    That way you will be able to pick and choose what you like while abandoning elements of the logo that you could do without.

  2. Choose the top three designs from your sketchbook and implement them into Adobe Illustrator.

    Remember, the design should be simple, recognizable, and look good in any orientation. (Huge bonus if it has symmetrical elements.)

    Different famous brand logos from all over.

  3. After tracing your design on adoble illustartor witht the Pen tool you are going to color it.

    Keep your design symmetrical and stick with black and white for colors.

  4. Establish the colors of your logo.

    Test run multple different color schemes for your logo and choose the best. Stick to 2-3 colors as any more than that may get too busy.

  5. Export your logo for Web, and Print.

    For Web: Go to Export > Save for web / For Print: change color palette to a Pantone swatchbook and export as PNG.
